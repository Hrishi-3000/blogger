Why I Started

Beyond the textbook paragraph.

History is often reduced to a few lines, leaving behind countless stories that deserve to be remembered.

My blogs exist to recover that context — explaining not just what happened, but why it mattered — and to encourage readers to look past surface-level narratives. Whether it's the rise of ancient Indian dynasties, the bravery of Param Vir Chakra recipients, the treaties that redrew borders, or the movements that shaped nations, every article is built to make history engaging and genuinely accessible.
